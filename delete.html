Account Deletion – NASIYA OL

If you would like to permanently delete your NASIYA OL account and all associated data, please follow the steps below.

How to Request Account Deletion

  • Open the NASIYA OL mobile app
  • Go to your Profile section
  • Tap "Delete Account"
  • Confirm your deletion request

Alternatively, you can send a deletion request via email:

Email: nasiyaol@gmail.com

Subject: Account Deletion Request

Phone: +998 77 789 59 50

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • User profile information (name, email, phone)
  • Listings and uploaded images
  • Chat and communication history
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers and FCM token

Data Retention

Some transactional or legally required records may be retained for up to 30 days to comply with legal obligations or prevent fraud. After this period, the data will be permanently deleted.
